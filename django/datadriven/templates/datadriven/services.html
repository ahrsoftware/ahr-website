The Process

We understand how dynamic and unpredictable research projects can be. We anticipate and accommodate this unpredictability with a process that's transparent, flexible, and straightforward.

Although each project is unique, the below diagram shows the lifecycle of a typical project:

  1. Advice (free, no obligation, grant app)
  2. In-depth discussion to establish and agree on core requirements
  3. Early designs, discussed for feedback
  4. Iterative development, with regular reviews to accommodate unforeseen changes
  5. Quality checks: tests, checks, feedback, tweaks
  6. Make live
  7. Maintenance and open communication
