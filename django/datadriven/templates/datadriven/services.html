The Process

We understand how dynamic and unpredictable research projects can be. We anticipate and accommodate this unpredictability with a process that's transparent, flexible, and straightforward.

Although each project is unique, a typical project lifecycle follows these 5 steps:

  1. Initial free advice session and help with grant application
  2. Establish and agree on requirements
  3. Iterative design and development, with regular reviews to accommodate unforeseen changes
  4. Finish: final quality checks, tests, make live/public
  5. Ongoing maintenance and open communication
